Go to https://choosealicense.com

Public Domain
	Public domain is a license that says everyone owns it.
	Examples include: CC0, The Unlicense, WTFPL
	
Open Source Permissive
	A license that specifies one entity that owns it.
	Anyone who gets their hands on it can do anything they want except for a handfull of restrictions.
	Permissive means you are allowed to make and distribute closed source versions.
	Examples include: MIT, BSD, Apache, CC-BY, zlib/libpng, beerware
	
Open Source Restricive / Copyleft
	There is a copyright license that says it's owned by the free software foundation.
	Anyone can do anything with it as long as the license stays on it and they always distribute changes.
	Examples include: GPL, CC-BY-SA, LGPL
